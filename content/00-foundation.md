## Grammar notes

### Counter words (classifiers)
Bangla attaches a small suffix to numbers to say *what* you are counting:

- **-ta** for objects (default classifier): `ak-ta chhata` = an umbrella, `dosh-ta boi` = ten books
- **-jon** for people: `ak-jon lok` = one man, `baro-jon manush` = twelve people
- For units (kg, ghonta, mash, bochhor), you usually skip the classifier: `pach kg`, `dui mash`.

### No plural with numbers
You do not pluralise the noun when a number is present: `dui mash` (two month), not `dui mash-gula`.

### Plural marker
For plural without a number, append **-gula**: `boi-gula` = the books.
